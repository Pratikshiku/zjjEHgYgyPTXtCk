## Cat Cafe Pet Coffee Shop Platform

> [Click me download source code / Try it online](https://www.devquizdone.online/detail/74bc7036e9fb4b50832f836bdc6bdbbb/ghb20250919) 

### 1. Related Technologies
- Backend: `Java`, `JavaWeb` / `Springboot`
- Frontend: `Vue`, `HTML` / `CSS` / `Javascript`, etc.
- Database: `MySQL`

### 2. Related Software (Any one of the listed software can be used)
- `IDEA`
- `Eclipse`
- `Visual Studio Code(VScode)`
- `Navicat`
- etc.

### 3. Function Description
The system is divided into (roles): Regular Users, Caretakers, Administrators.

**Regular User Functions:**
1. Login
2. Registration
3. Homepage
4. Coffee Menu
5. Pet Information
6. Merchandise
7. Personal Center
8. My Favorites
9. Change Password
10. Personal Information
11. Coffee Menu
12. Menu Orders
13. Pet Experience
14. Pet Orders
15. Pet Boarding
16. Health Status
17. Care Services
18. Product Purchase
19. My Favorites Management


**Caretaker Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. Change Password
4. Personal Information
5. Pet Experience
6. Pet Boarding
7. Health Status
8. Care Services



**Administrator Functions:**
1. Login
2. Personal Center
3. User Management
4. Caretaker Management
5. Coffee Menu Management
6. Menu Type Management
7. Menu Order Management
8. Pet Information Management
9. Pet Experience Management
10. Pet Order Management
11. Pet Boarding Management
12. Health Status Management
13. Interactive Activity Management
14. Care Service Management
15. Merchandise Management
16. Product Type Management
17. Product Purchase Management
18. Product Inventory Management
19. Banner Management
